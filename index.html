Sha'eris

Adventurer of Faerûn and Party Leader.

Somewhere in Faerûn, most recently squatting in the Lower City.

'Wild'-ly talented and generally neighbourly, looking for a role as a soldier of fortune in which I can bring fresh ideas and perspectives while assisting to bring your plans (from world domination to saving a pet) to life!


Key Facts:

Seldarine Drow.

Lvl 9 Sorcerer - Wild Magic, Lvl 3 Warlock - The Fiend.

Background: Urchin.

Patron: The Fiend, likely an Archdevil or Demon Lord.


Positives:

Not fully Illithid!

Not the evil kind of drow!

Have the tendancy to turn people into cats accidentally!

Negatives:

Almost Illithid :(


Experience:

- Escaped captivity on the Nautiloid led by a group of Illithids/Mind Flayers.

- Protected the Emerald Grove (otherwise known as Silvanus' Grove or the Druid's Grove) against the Absolutist-cult-following goblins and restore proper leadership from the corrupt government.

- Saved two companions from their cults, neutral-evil aligned deity Shar (also known as the Nightdinger) and evil aligned Vlaakith CLVII (also known as the Lich Queen and ruler of the githyanki race).

- Lifted the eternal darkness curse from the Shadowlands

- Defeated Ketheric Thorm, a chosen of the deity Myrkul (God of Decay and Exhaustion), and one of Myrkul's physical forms.
